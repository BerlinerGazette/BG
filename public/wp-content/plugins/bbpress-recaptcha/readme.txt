This is a simple plugin that adds a recaptcha validation to the bbPress 2.0 topic creation and topic reply forum.

== Description ==

Sick of getting spam on your bbPress 2.0 forums? This will add a reCaptcha anti-bot system to your new topic  and topic reply forums.

It's very simple to use. Just activate, then go to Settings > bbPress reCaptcha and enter your Public and Private keys.

== Installation ==

1. Upload bbpress-recaptcha to wp-content/plugins
2. Click "Activate" in the WordPress plugins menu
3. Go to Settings > bbPress reCaptcha and enter your public and private keys
